List HubCell Data
      Usage:
            1. bc-lhcd
            2. bc-lhcd filtertext
            3. bc-lhcd x y
            4. bc-lhcd x y filtertext

            filtertext is a string to compare against prefab names
            x y are the grid co-ords for the cells, 0 0 being the central hub, out to 6 for the limit on a vanilla world
